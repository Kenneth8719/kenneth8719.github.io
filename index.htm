Innovation Driven, Technology First

China Enertech Industrial Development Co., Ltd. is an international advanced ceramics enterprise specializing in the research, development, and manufacturing of high-performance ceramic materials. Established in April 2017 and headquartered in Hong Kong, the company has rapidly evolved into a significant player in the global ceramics industry through continuous innovation and strategic expansion.

Initially focused on energy-saving industrial ceramics, Enertech has built a strong technological foundation in material science and engineering applications. In 2020, the company strategically entered the high-growth dental ceramics sector, leveraging its core expertise to produce and distribute premium dental zirconia and lithium disilicate ceramics. These products are widely recognized for their superior strength, biocompatibility, and aesthetic properties, meeting the exacting demands of modern restorative dentistry.

With multiple specialized manufacturing facilities across Mainland China, Enertech operates an integrated supply chain that ensures stringent quality control, scalable production, and consistent supply to markets worldwide. Committed to sustainability and innovation, Enertech invests significantly in R&D to advance material technologies and develop new applications across industrial and biomedical fields. By combining advanced manufacturing capabilities with a customer-oriented approach, China Enertech continues to strengthen its global presence, driving progress in ceramic technology and contributing to a more efficient and healthier world
